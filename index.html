Welcome to Lonefa Integrated Farms Limited

Lonefa Integrated Farms Limited

Founded in July, 2020, Lonefa Integrated Farms has the overall goal of producing nutritious livestock and crops for food, alleviating poverty and hunger by providing employment to young people thereby promoting well being, excellent health and longevity in tandem with United Nation’s Sustainable Development Goals 1 2 3.

WHAT WE DO

Lonefa Integrated Farm is poised to revolutionize farming and agricultural practice by developing quality products in a hygienic environment, with affordable prices for local, national and international consumption.

Training

Train, coach and mentor young adults in various aspects of agriculture

Employment

Create wealth and reduce poverty by providing employment and practical experience in the farm.

Food Production

Produce high quality and organic meat products, eggs and basic food crops

Feed Compounding and Production

Adapt the use of modern techniques and machines for feeds compounding and production to optimize livestock performance.

Quality Farm Produce

Produce high quality and organic meat products, eggs and basic food crops.
