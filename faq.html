    FAQs

      FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

      • When was Lonefa Inaugrated?

        Lonefa Was Inaugrated on the 29th of July 2020 with the Vital Aim of Supporting the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals (SDG 1 2 3) by Eradicating Poverty Through the Provision of Employment, Encouraging Zero Hunger Through the Production of Nutritious and Healthy Crops and Livestock which will Feed The Teeming Population and Promoting good Health and Well-being.

      • What is the Aim of Lonefa?

        Seeking for ways to fight poverty and create employment for youths especially, Teenagers Support Organization (T.S.O) birthed an initiative known as LONEFA Integrated Farm. LONEFA is an acronym for Love Never Fails. The initiative (farm), in support of the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals seeks to support SDG 1, 2 and 3 by eradicating poverty through the provision of employment, fighting against hunger through the production of nutritious crops and livestock which will feed the populace

      • How Many Employees/Workers does Lonefa Have?

        Lonefa has in the Last 1 year Trained and employed over 50 young Adults and youths in Agriculture and Farming.

      • Does Lonefa Train Young Adults and youths in Agriculture?

        At LONEFA Integrated Farms LTD, we are dedicated to every aspect of the farming process, providing our customers with quality farm produce [Fish, Pigs, Cows, Goats, Crops, Egg production and Feed Production]. We aim to create job opportunities for youths
