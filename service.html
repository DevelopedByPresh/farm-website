Our Services

Training

Train, coach and mentor young adults in various aspects of agriculture.

Employment

Create wealth and reduce poverty by providing employment and practical experience in the farm.

Feed Production

Adapt the use of modern techniques and machines for feeds compounding and production to optimize livestock performance.

Food Production

We Produce high quality and organic meat products, eggs and basic food crops. Lonefa Farms has in the last one year produced different Livestock and crops which has Boosted the food production capacity of Rivers state and is Expanding its capacity in all Frontiers.

Eradicating Hunger

We Strive To Achieve the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals (SDG 1 2 3) by Eradicating Poverty, Encouraging Zero Hunger Through the Production of Nutritious and Healthy Crops and Livestock.

Creating Jobs

Lonefa Integrated Farms Trains and Encourages Young Adults in Agriculture.. We aim to create job opportunities for youths.

A Foremost global source for the healthy food supply chain and a platform for youth socio-economic development.
